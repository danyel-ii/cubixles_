# Support

Last updated: 2025-12-26

## Bug reports
- Open an issue with a minimal reproduction and environment details.
- Include logs or screenshots when relevant.

## Feature requests
- Open an issue describing the problem, proposed solution, and alternatives.

## Security issues
Do not open a public issue. See `SECURITY.md`.

## Response targets
- Bugs: response within 7 business days
- Feature requests: response within 14 business days
